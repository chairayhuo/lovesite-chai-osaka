Our story begins..

Once upon a time, there was a brave little girl who loved adventures. She was very proud and sure of herself, but she was scared to make a promise to stay with someone forever. There was also a cheerful little boy who was kind and always saw the bright side, but he felt lonely and really wanted to be loved a lot.

One special day, they bumped into each other. It felt like they had been friends for a very long time because they were so comfy and knew each other so well. They were like mirrors, showing each other what they were like. They held hands and walked forward together, ready to be the best they could be and to love each other lots.
